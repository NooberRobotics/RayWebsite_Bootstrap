objective

ENPH 253

This is an annual 5 credit course offered by UBC as part of the standard timetable of the Engineering Physics program. The students go together in gropus of four and construct an autnonus robot to enter in a competition at the end of the term. The nature of the competition is different every year.

2016 competition

The competition of this year was set up as follows: traverse the map to the left looking for passengers to pick up and transport to the dropp-off area marked in gray. Potential passengers stand on the side of the curb on a base emitting IR light with a 1kHz frequency. Two vehicles will be on the track at the same time. In the event of a collision, the robot must back away from the impact within 2 seconds. The robot that dropps off the most dolls within two minutes winns the round.
